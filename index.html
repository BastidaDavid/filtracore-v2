System Guide

FiltraCore User Manual

Quick guide for registering machines, inventory, filters, PSI readings, and reading alerts.

1. Dashboard

Use the dashboard to review operational status, filter costs, risk exposure, potential savings, and intelligence alerts.

2. Machines

Register each machine that uses filters. Add name, type, and location so every filter can be connected to the correct equipment.

3. Inventory

Add filter products, stock quantity, unit cost, reorder level, and category. Inventory stock decreases when a filter is assigned.

4. Filters

Assign a filter product to a machine. FiltraCore calculates lifecycle status, due date, cost, and PSI condition.

5. PSI Monitoring

Enter current PSI when assigning or updating a filter. Healthy PSI is 50–70, warning is 35–49, and critical is 0–34.

6. Intelligence Alerts

Open Intelligence Alerts to review active risk, critical warnings, estimated financial exposure, and recommended actions.

7. Financial Metrics

Total spend tracks installed filter cost. Risk Exposure estimates operational risk. Potential Savings estimates avoided risk if issues are handled on time.
